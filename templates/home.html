Credit risk analysis is a process that lenders and financial institutions use to evaluate the likelihood of borrowers defaulting on their credit obligations, assess potential risks associated with lending, and make informed decisions to manage and minimize credit-related losses. Credit Risk Analysis is an important process that enables lenders, credit rating agencies, and other financial institutions to evaluate the creditworthiness of borrowers and make informed decisions about extending credit. In this project, we will be using the XGBoost algorithm to predict whether a borrower is likely to default on a loan or not. It involves analyzing various factors such as the borrower's financial health, credit history, repayment capacity, market conditions, and other relevant information to determine the borrower's creditworthiness and potential risks. Based on their analysis, lenders can make informed decisions on loan approvals, credit limits, interest rates, and other credit-related terms and conditions to minimize credit-related losses.
